    (Watch Trailer)

    "The Shawshank Redemption" is Set in the 1940s, the story follows Andy Dufresne, a successful banker who is wrongfully convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. Sentenced to two consecutive life terms at Shawshank State Prison, Andy befriends fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding, who becomes his closest ally.

  3. Avatar (2009)
    (Watch Trailer)

    Directed by James Cameron, "Avatar" is set in the lush alien world of Pandora. The story follows Jake Sully, a paraplegic Marine who becomes part of the Avatar Program. As he grows close to the native Na'vi people, Jake must choose between following orders or defending the world he's come to love.

  4. The Godfather (1972)
    (Watch Trailer)

    "The Godfather" is a classic film that was released on March 15, 1972, directed by Francis Ford Coppola and based on Mario Puzo's novel. The film is a cornerstone of American cinema, exploring the complexities of the Mafia through the lens of the fictional Corleone family.

  5. The Dark Knight (2008)
    (Watch Trailer)

    "The Dark Knight" The film opens with a bank heist orchestrated by the Joker, who eliminates his own gang members to claim the loot. As Gotham City faces increasing chaos, Batman teams up with District Attorney Harvey Dent and Commissioner Gordon to dismantle organized crime. However, the Joker emerges as a formidable adversary, seeking to undermine Batman's influence and plunge the city into anarchy.
